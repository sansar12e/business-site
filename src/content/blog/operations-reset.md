Operational drag often appears as a series of small annoyances that slowly erode focus and speed.

## 1. Decisions are delayed

If every choice needs multiple approvals, the work will feel heavy. Clear ownership often solves a surprising amount of friction.

## 2. Hand-offs create confusion

Frequent context loss between teams is a strong sign that the system needs redesign rather than more effort.

## 3. People are over-functioning

When the team is constantly firefighting, a reset may be the fastest path to better results.
